Visualizing FDA data

Visualizing data for adverse drug reactions

The graphical analysis presents the top ten adverse reactions reported.

While the reports to FDA do not require that a causal relationship between a product and event be proven, and reports do not always contain enough detail to properly evaluate an event, we have provided the drill down on each adverse reaction to reflect the occurrence of SUSPECT drug reported in the adverse action report.

We have further provided drill down on each drug to show the gender wise impact reported for the drug.
